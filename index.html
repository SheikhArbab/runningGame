About us

The Al Junaibi Legacy Founded in 2011 by the visionary Mr. Saeed Al Junaibi, the S.A.J Holding & Investment has established itself as a force to be reckoned with in the global business landscape. Driven by a passion for innovation and a commitment to excellence, our journey began with a singular vision: to redefine the boundaries of what's possible.
Diverse Ventures, Unified Vision
Our diverse portfolio boasts a unique blend of enterprises, each marked by cutting-edge technology, unparalleled craftsmanship, and a drive to push the envelope. At the heart of our conglomerate:

  • • Ai Techniques Subsidiary: A beacon of technological advancements, continually setting industry benchmarks and championing groundbreaking innovations.
  • • Automotive Customization: Riding the wave of automotive excellence, Cardip marries precision engineering with avant-garde designs, keeping us at the forefront of the automotive evolution.
  • • EVS: Our electric vehicle venture, EVS, embodies our commitment to a sustainable future. A testament to our foresight, it promises a world where mobility is clean, efficient, and revolutionary.
